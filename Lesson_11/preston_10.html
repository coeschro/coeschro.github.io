Idaho Preferred

Preston Berry Farms was the first to introduce high tunnel production of juicy red raspberries in South Eastern Idaho and folks are coming from all over to take a look! Located in the small friendly town of Preston, Idaho, passer-bys often pull off to the side of the road to look at the magnificence of the brambles inside their canopies. Protected from wind, precipitation, and those nasty bugs, the plants live a stress free life. Preston Berry Farms is Idaho Preferred. At harvest time they yield up their big beautiful fruit that is loaded with all of natures medicine in the sweetest way. Their products include raspberries, raspberry jam, seedless jam, sauce and drizzle, blackberries and strawberries.
